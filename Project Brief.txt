The Situation
I am a new data analyst at MAVEN TOYS, which is a Toy store chain with multiple store locations in Mexico

The Assignment
I have access to data containing transactional records from January 2022 - September 2023, along with the information about products and store locations
My goal is to build a simple, interactive report that the leadership team can use to monitor key business metrices and high level trends.

The Objectives
1. Connect and Profile the Data
2. Create a relational model
3. Add calculated measures and fields
4. Build an interactive report
